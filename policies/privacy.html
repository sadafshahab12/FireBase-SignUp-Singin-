Privacy Policy for Login System

Last Updated: 30-december-2024

At Code & Connect, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal data we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it secure when you use our login system.

  • Information We Collect When you use our login system, we may collect the following types of information:
    • Personal Information: Your name, email address, and any other information provided during registration or login. Login Credentials: Your username and password (encrypted for security).
    • Usage Data: Information about how you use our system, such as the time and duration of your login sessions, pages viewed, and interaction with our platform.

    • How We Use Your Information

  • We use your information to: Provide you with access to your account and personalized features. Enhance the security of our login system by monitoring and preventing unauthorized access. Communicate with you about your account or changes to our services. Improve our website and services based on user feedback and activity.
  • Data Security: We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information, including: Encryption of passwords. Secure servers and SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) technology. Regular security audits and updates to ensure protection from unauthorized access.

    • Data Sharing

  • We do not share or sell your personal information to third parties, except in the following cases:
    • Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our website and services.
    • Legal Compliance: If required by law, we may disclose your personal information to comply with legal obligations, protect our rights, or respond to legal processes.
  • Your Rights You have the right to: Access the information we hold about you. Request the deletion of your account and personal data. Correct any inaccuracies in your personal information.
  • Changes to This Privacy Policy: We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the updated policy on our website.
